Weight Gain Tips

Follow these guidelines for maximum muscle growth

Eat Surplus Calories

Consume 300-500 calories above maintenance to support muscle growth. Focus on protein-rich foods.

Rest & Recover

Muscles grow during rest, not workouts. Get 7-9 hours of sleep and rest days between training sessions.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase weight, reps, or sets each week to continuously challenge your muscles.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily. Proper hydration is essential for muscle function and recovery.
